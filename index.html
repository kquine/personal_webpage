About

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH). I study automatic formal analysis and software engineering methods to develop safe and reliable computer systems.

Research Interests

  • Integration of formal analysis, robust system design, and software engineering approaches to develop safe, reliable, and secure hardware, software, and cyber-physical systems
  • Scalable yet precise automatic analysis techniques, based on model checking, SMT, and computational logic

Tools

  • Maude LTLR Model Checker
  • SynchAADL2Maude tool
  • Maude LTL LMC Model Checker
